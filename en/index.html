Who We Are

One Africa.
Many Voices.

Our Pan-African Club brings together students from across Africa and the diaspora to celebrate culture, leadership, education, and unity at Istanbul Medipol University.

Our Mission

To create a strong student community that promotes African heritage, supports international students, and builds leadership through culture, education, and collaboration.
